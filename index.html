> What is Jmpr?

Jmpr is a 3d, virtual reality, platformer. (a game!)

> How to play

Move your phone as if it were looking through the eyes of the character. Then, either tap the phone or pull the magnet on the side of your VR device (Google Cardboard, Galaxy Vr, etc) to jump in the direction you are looking.

> How to install

Transfer the ".apk" file onto your android device, click the file to install the game. You'll need to enable USB debugging for this to work. This link has more information on the topic.
